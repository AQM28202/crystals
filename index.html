The Crystal Collector

Rules to the Game

A random number will be displayed at the beginning of the game.


Clicking on a crystal will add a certain amount of points to your total score.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The values of each crystal will change each time the game restarts.
